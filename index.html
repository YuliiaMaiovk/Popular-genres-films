Comedy

Comedy is a story that tells about a series of funny, or comical events, intended to make the audience laugh. It is a very open genre, and thus crosses over with many other genres on a frequent basis.

Fantasy

A fantasy story is about magic or supernatural forces, rather than technology (as science fiction) if it happens to take place in a modern or future era. Depending on the extent of these other elements, the story may or may not be considered to be a "hybrid genre" series.

Horrors

A horror story is told to deliberately scare or frighten the audience, through suspense or shock. Horror may also overlap with the fantasy, supernatural fiction, and thriller genres.
